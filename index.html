Brief Biography

I am currently a Ph.D candidate at School of Information Science and Engineering, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, China. My main research interests lie in multimodal fusion and embodied intelligence. I pursued my Ph.D. degree from School of Information Science and Engineering, Wuhan University of Science and Technology, China, in 2022, supervised by Prof. Huasong Min, Prof. Bin Fang, and Prof. Fuchun Sun. I am also a member in Institute of Robotics and Intelligent Systems. Before my Ph.D. study, I received my B.E. degree and M.S. degree from School of Mechanical Engineering, Wuhan University of Textile, China, in 2019、2022.
